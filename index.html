Welcome to my website

My name is Arne Soyez and I study HBO-ICT at the HZ in Middelburg. I had an interest in computers when I was younger and by following this course I hope to understand more of how computers work and to be able to make my own programs and other things one day. I chose this course so I can learn more about programming and scripting because I enjoy working with computers.


My foster father introduced me to ICT when I was 12 years old. He had his own IT company where he designed websites, hosted websites for other people and other things from his home. He told me a lot about it and I was immediately interested. I have also done an internship once at an IT company which made me more interested to do something with computers. IT jobs can earn you a decent amount of money and if you are able to make your own game for example it can also be fun. I have an idea of a game I want to make so by following this course I hope that one day I will be able to actually realise my idea.
