My motivation
My motivation for this course is that I had an interest in computers when I was younger. By following this course I hope to understand more of how computers work and to be able to make my own programs and other things one day. Another thing that motivates me for this course is that IT jobs can earn you a decent amount of money and if you are able to build your own game for example it can also be fun. Being able to script can also give you the opportunity to let your creativity run wild. I also know some other people that work or have worked in IT, and I have also done an internship once at an IT company which made me more interested to do something with computers.
