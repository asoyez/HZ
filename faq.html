Frequently asked questions

You have to put money on your schoolpass and then you can go to the printer. At the printer you have to scan your schoolpass and select the document you want to print. You can send your document via print.hz.

You have to put money on your schoolpass and go to a printer. At the printer you can scan your schoolpass and select the document you want to send to your laptop. Then press select and it will send the document to your laptop.

Go to the HZ webshop and login. Then select the item you want and add it to your cart. Go to your shopping cart and click on checkout. Follow the remaining instructions.

If you are sick or show symptoms of COVID you need to go to the HZ website, click on 'Current Affairs' and there you will find information and the latest updates about the coronavirus.

To book a project room you have to log in on your HZ account and hover over services and click on helpdesk / IT facilities. Then click on 'Make a reservation' and you will enter a new page. There you can book a project room.

Go to the HZ website and click on 'About HZ'. Then hover over locations and chose the location of your school. You will enter a new page where you can find all the information about the HZ parking lot.
